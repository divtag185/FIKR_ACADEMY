About Us

A Different Kind of Learning Experience

At FIKR Integrated Academy, we believe education should prepare children not only for examinations, but for life, leadership, and success in the Hereafter. We integrate Islamic values, academic excellence, technology, and character development into the learner's daily experience — not as separate add-ons, but as one coherent whole.

Faith

Strong Islamic foundation woven into every dimension of school life.

Knowledge

Academic excellence through the Nigerian curriculum, taught for deep understanding.

Innovation

Technology integrated from an early stage — learners become creators, not consumers.

Responsibility

Character, moral courage and service to community built alongside academics.

Why Fikr Academy

Where Faith, Knowledge &
Technology Meet

Rather than treating Faith, Knowledge, Innovation, and Responsibility as separate programmes, FIKR integrates them into the learner's daily educational experience.

Mathematics Mastery

We emphasise understanding before memorisation — developing number sense, logical thinking, problem-solving, and real-life application skills, not just exam passes.

Islamic Excellence Programme

Qur'an, Tajweed, Arabic, Seerah, daily Adhkaar, and Islamic manners are structured into school life — nurturing learners who are confident in faith and guided by sound values.

Technology & Future Skills

From digital literacy to coding foundations and computational thinking, learners are equipped to become innovators and problem-solvers in the digital world.

Strong Literacy Programme

Reading fluency, vocabulary, writing, public speaking, and critical reading form the backbone of all learning — producing confident readers, writers, and communicators.

Nigerian Curriculum

FIKR follows the Nigerian curriculum while going deeper — emphasising genuine understanding, critical thinking, and mastery across all approved subjects.

Small Learning Communities

Smaller class sizes, close learner support, mentorship, and strong parent communication mean every child gets the individual attention they deserve.

Academics

World-Class Education

FIKR follows the Nigerian curriculum while emphasising deeper understanding and mastery. Learning is designed to encourage critical thinking, communication, and genuine problem-solving — not just examination success.

Nursery School Programme

A warm, nurturing start to learning where young children develop early literacy, numeracy, social skills, and a love for the Qur'an through play-based and hands-on activities in a safe, caring environment.

Social Skills, Independence & Emotional Development

Technology & Innovation

Building Creators,
Not Just Consumers

Technology is integrated into learning from an early stage at FIKR. We want learners to become creators, innovators, and problem-solvers — not passive consumers of technology.

Digital Literacy & Internet Research

Students learn responsible, informed digital citizenship and how to navigate and evaluate online information effectively.

Coding Foundations & Computational Thinking

Introducing programming logic and problem-solving through code from a young age — building the mindset of a builder, not a user.

Productivity & Presentation Skills

Responsible Technology Use

Every learner understands the ethics, boundaries, and responsibilities that come with living and working in a digital world.

Admission Process

We welcome families who share our values of faith, knowledge, and a genuine commitment to their children's future. Our admissions process is designed to be clear, supportive, and straightforward.

1

Enquiry & Initial Contact

Submit the enquiry form, call us or send a WhatsApp message. Our admissions team will respond within 24 hours.

2

School Visit & Tour

We invite you to visit the school, meet the team and see the learning environment for yourself.

3

Entry Assessment

A friendly, age-appropriate assessment helps us understand your child's current level and learning needs.

4

Offer & Enrolment

Once a place is offered, complete enrolment paperwork and secure your child's place. Welcome to the Fikr family!
